Breakfast Smoothie

Description

A tasty, simple quick breakfest smoothie if you don't feel like cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of milk
  • 1 Banana
  • 2 Tablespoon of peanut butter
  • 1/2 Cup of oatmeal

Directions

  1. Pour milk into blender
  2. Pour oatmeal into blender
  3. Add banana.
  4. Add peanut butter
  5. Blend
  6. Enjoy
